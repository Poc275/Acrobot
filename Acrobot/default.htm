HCI Bot

Beginnings of the HCI bot. For now it's based on a simple user story of finding acronyms. This is done through LUIS but you can also chat with the bot to see sentiment analysis in action.

Some example interactions:

  • What does EEC stand for?
  • What does ETOPS mean?
  • TLA = Three Letter Acronym
  • BRB stands for Be Right Back
  • Thanks for your help (sentiment analysis)
